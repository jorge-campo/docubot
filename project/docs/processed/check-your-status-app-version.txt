You can check the version of the Status app you're running to ensure it's up to date or for troubleshooting purposes.

## Check your Status app version

[Mobile Instructions]
1. Open the Status app on your mobile device.
1. Tap your profile picture, and then scroll down and tap "About".
1. The version of the Status app you're running is displayed next to "App version".
[Desktop Instructions]
1. Open the Status app on your desktop.
1. From the navigation sidebar, click "Settings" > "About".
1. The version of the Status app you're running is displayed above "Current version".
